Destinations

Great Western

If you are looking to climb the mountains with the sun shining on your face, then this is definetely the one for you! Temperatures here can sometimes exceed 45 Degrees so you better be prepared.

South Peak

The South peak is definetely our most popular destination due to most people finding it the most beautiful. The sunsets here are like no other and one of the interesting parts of the South Peak is that there is a very old tribe that has been living here for 50000 years.

North Dales

If you like long hikes, then you will love North Dales for sure! Here, we will be going on hikes that will be lasting as long as 36 hours. Don't worry, we will be setting up cam on the way.
